Market Movers
Australia / New Zealand
Australia's hotel market is running at 71% occupancy nationally, ADR around AUD 240, and RevPAR up 3.8% year-on-year with Sydney showing weekend compression (clean weekend ADR AUD 199 vs weekday AUD 140). Hotel News Resource, April 2026.

New Zealand faces an aviation capacity crunch. Air New Zealand cut approximately 1,100 flights between March 16 and May 3, 2026 (about 5% of services), affecting around 44,000 passengers. The Auckland-Wellington round trip is now running 8-12% above April fares. Christchurch loses the most frequency: 27 Auckland rotations and 30 Wellington services cut through May-June. Travel Tourister, April 29 2026 | Travel Tourister, April 30 2026.

New Zealand's government announced NZD 30 million in regional aviation support to stabilize inter-island routes. When NZ domestic air capacity tightens, hotel demand in destination markets strengthens. Travel and Tour World, May 2026.
Japan
Golden Week (late April to early May) delivered peak hotel demand as expected. Tokyo hotels are running at 91% occupancy with average daily rates of JPY 18,965 (approximately USD 135). Hotel supply remains constrained by rising construction costs and a persistent labor shortage, supporting rate integrity. Travel and Tour World, May 2026.

Full-year 2026 inbound is forecast down 3% to 41.4 million due to fewer Chinese and Hong Kong visitors, but total spend is projected up 0.6% to JPY 9.6 trillion as European, US, and Australian visitors who stay longer and spend more fill the gap. Travel Voice, 2026. Note: inbound down but per-head spend up signals premium demand. Your luxury Accor properties (Fairmont, Sofitel, Pullman Japan) benefit.
Korea
Korea's 2026 golden holiday is bigger than prior years. May 1 (Labour Day) was designated a statutory holiday for the first time, creating a five-day break through Children's Day (May 5). Grand Hyatt Jeju reached up to 97% occupancy of its 1,600 rooms at peak days, with over 70% foreign guests. Paradise City Incheon exceeded 90% occupancy of its 769 rooms. Chinese tourists to Korea rose 22-32% over last year during the break, with Japanese tourists up 18-20%. Travel and Tour World, May 2026.

Seoul hotel demand continues to attract major chain investment. South Korea has become a competitive battleground for Hilton, Marriott, and Accor mid-scale brands. KED Global, April 2026.
Greater China (incl. HK / Taiwan)
Marriott Q1 2026 data (reported May 6) shows Greater China RevPAR up approximately 6%, with Hong Kong and Hainan each near plus 20%. China domestic May Day demand hit a record: the Ministry of Culture and Tourism tracked over 1.52 billion domestic trips during the five-day break. Outbound is accelerating (bookings up 53%), but inbound international demand into China's own hotels is a mixed story with ongoing geopolitical headwinds. CoStar, May 6 2026 | MICE Travel Advisor, May 2026.
Southeast Asia (Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines)
Vietnam: 8.8 million international arrivals January-April, up 15% year-on-year. First four consecutive months above 2 million. Top markets: China and South Korea (39.8% combined). Vietnam has already achieved 35% of its 25 million target for full-year 2026. Agoda reported an 81% surge in domestic accommodation searches among Vietnamese travelers for the April long break. VnExpress, May 3 2026 | PRNewswire/Agoda, May 2026.

Thailand: TAT forecast 136,000 Chinese visitors April 29-May 6, up 26% year-on-year, generating THB 3.9 billion (up 27%). Thailand climbed from 5% to 9% share of Chinese May Day outbound, overtaking Japan. Low-season hotel bookings are separately reported down 20-30% versus last year, signaling a sharp demand cliff post-holiday. Membership propositions that drive shoulder-period stays are well-timed here. Nation Thailand, May 2 2026.

Malaysia: Has overtaken Thailand as most-visited destination in Southeast Asia in early 2026. Budget 2026 allocated RM 700 million for tourism promotion. Visit Malaysia 2026 campaign, ASEAN Summit, and SEA Games underpin sustained demand through the year. Travel and Tour World, May 2026.

Singapore: Traveloka's 5.5 EPIC Sale (May 4-8) shows Southeast Asia's "double-digit date" behavior is real: platform data shows a 25% transaction premium on dates like 5.5 versus surrounding days even without promotional discounts. Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam customer bases showed up to 70% transaction value growth during comparable promotions. Manila Times / PRNewswire, April 30 2026.

Philippines: Continues to underperform regional peers. International arrivals remain the lowest among major SEA nations, still well below 2019 levels. Travel and Tour World, May 2026.
India
Hotel room rates in India are forecast to rise 4-6% in 2026 to INR 9,400-9,700, driven by domestic tourism growth and corporate travel demand outpacing supply. New Delhi, Mumbai, and Chandigarh maintained high occupancy through Q1 2026 despite regional geopolitical noise. The 2026 calendar has an unusually high number of long weekends, stimulating short-haul leisure. Mid-scale and upper-midscale hotels make up 50%+ of the pipeline. Business Standard, 2026.
